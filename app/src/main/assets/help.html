EPS Viewer Help

Welcome!

EPS Viewer is a fast, offline EPS file viewer and converter for Android.

All processing happens on your device—no files are uploaded to the internet.

Features

  • Open EPS files from your device or cloud storage
  • Fast, accurate preview with zoom and pan
  • Export to PNG, JPG, or PDF format
  • Adjust resolution for different use cases
  • View recent files for quick access
  • 100% offline—no internet required

Getting Started

  1. Open a File: Tap "Open EPS File" to select an EPS from your device
  2. View: Use zoom, pan, and fit-to-screen controls
  3. Export: Tap "Export" to save as PNG, JPG, or PDF

Free vs Premium

Free: Standard resolution exports (150 DPI), ads on home screen

Premium: High-resolution exports (300–600 DPI), batch conversion, no ads

Or watch a rewarded ad for a one-time high-resolution export!

Export Options

  • PNG: Lossless format, good for editing
  • JPG: Compressed format, smaller file size
  • PDF: Portable document format (Premium only)

Higher DPI = larger file, better quality. Choose based on your use case:

  • 150 DPI: Screen viewing
  • 300 DPI: Standard printing
  • 600 DPI: High-quality printing

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • + Zoom in
  • − Zoom out
  • Fit Fit to screen
  • 100% Actual size
Privacy: All file processing happens on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your files on any server. Your EPS files remain completely private.

Troubleshooting

File won't open? Ensure it's a valid EPS file. Try a different file.

Export failed? Check available storage and file permissions.

App is slow? Restart the app or clear cache in Settings.
