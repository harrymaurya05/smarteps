Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

EPS Viewer (Offline) ("App") is designed with your privacy as our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our App.

Key principle: We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or your EPS files to any external server.

2. Offline-First Design

All rendering, conversion, and processing of EPS files occurs entirely on your device. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party service.

We use a locally-embedded EPS renderer (based on open-source technology) that works completely offline.

3. Permissions Explained

The App requests the following permissions and uses them only as described:

  • Read/Write Storage: To open EPS files from your device and save exports
  • Internet: Only for displaying ads and processing in-app purchases (both optional)
  • Access Network State: To check connectivity before attempting ads or billing

If you do not wish to see ads or make purchases, the App functions fully offline without using INTERNET permission.

4. What Data We Collect

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • Your EPS files or their contents
  • Personal information (name, email, phone, location, etc.)
  • Files metadata or thumbnails sent to servers
  • Usage logs or analytics that identify you

Data We MAY Collect (Optional):

  • Ad Impressions: Google Mobile Ads SDK displays non-personalized ads on free tier. Ads are served locally and do not track you personally.
  • Purchase Data: When you buy a premium subscription, Google Play Billing handles payment securely. We only see purchase confirmation, not payment details.
  • App Preferences: Your export format, DPI preferences, theme choice—stored locally only on your device in SharedPreferences.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the App: Process and render your EPS files
  • To provide ads: Show non-personalized ads (free tier only)
  • To process purchases: Validate premium subscriptions via Google Play
  • To improve: Understand which features users need (anonymously, where applicable)

6. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device, your data is protected by your device's security (lock screen, encryption, etc.). We do not transmit EPS file contents anywhere.

For purchases and ads, we use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS) and rely on Google Play Services security infrastructure.

7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following optional third-party services:

  • Google Mobile Ads SDK: For displaying ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: For in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy

These services are optional. You can use the App offline without them.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately.

9. Data Retention

Your Files: Stored only on your device. You control when they are deleted.

App Preferences: Stored locally. Uninstalling the App deletes them.

Ad/Purchase Data: Retained by Google per their policies.

10. Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is stored on your device; you control access.
  • Deletion: Clear App data in Settings, or uninstall the App.
  • Portability: Export your files from the App anytime.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or persistent identifiers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
